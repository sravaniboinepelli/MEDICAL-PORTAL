What is This?

This is a medical portal built by Sravani B and Ujwal , students of IIIT-H , as a part of their ITWS-2 course .

Features

A variety of features were implented . Some of them are listed below :

  • Generate Reports
  • Book Appointments
  • Find Doctors
  • Payment and ReImbursement
  • Patient Search

Generate Reports

Each patient can have their health record updated on a visit and said record can be downloaded in a pdf format.

Book Appointment

Each patient can book an appointment for a given date and time with a given doctor. An email will also be snet confirming the booking . Each Doctor view the appointments he has and when and with who

Find Doctor

Patients can search through a list of doctors and see which ones suit them the most

Payment and Reimbursement

Patient can pay their and also apply for reimbursements . The admin can approve or reject these requests.

Patient Search

The admin can search through the list of patients and see their history
